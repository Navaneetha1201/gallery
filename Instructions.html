Instructions


  1. The quizzes consists of questions carefully designed to help you self-assest on traditions of India. No data will be collected on the website regarding your responses or how many times you take the quiz.

  2. Click on the "start" button to start the game. First you have to select the states in India. There are 10 questions for each district. Each question you will provide 1 minute time within this time you have to answer.

  3. Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice or "true or false" format. Read each question carefully, and click on the button next button.

  4. After responding to a question, click on the "Next Question" button at the bottom to go to the next question. After responding to the 10th question, you can select another state to attend quizzes related to the traditions of that state.

  5. If you select an incorrect response for a question, it will show the incorrect answer with red and correct answer with green. If you retake the quiz, the questions and their respective responses will be randomized.

  6. The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all questions. If you respond incorrectly to a question or retake a question again and get the correct response, your quiz score will reflect it appropriately. However, your quiz will not be graded, if you skip a question or exit before responding to all the questions.


Note : The quizzes provided in this website is totally depend on the traditions of India.
